I am an aspiring data analyst completing my Master of Arts in Economics at the University of Florida, specializing in econometrics and sports analytics. 

My unique perspective comes from blending hands-on football operations experience with advanced statistical modeling. I bridge the gap between complex data and actionable strategy—whether analyzing player tracking data for roster construction, building logistic drive-level probability models, or coordinating equipment logistics for a 200+ person roster on game day.

### Technical Skillset
* **Languages & Software:** R (`cfbfastR`, `tidyverse`), Python, SQL, Excel, Tableau, PowerPoint, Stata, Google Colab
* **Methodologies:** Linear & Logistic Regression, Variable Selection (LASSO, AIC Backward Elimination), Multicollinearity Diagnosis (VIF), Spatial Tracking Analysis

### Core Areas of Focus
* **Football Analytics:** Player evaluation trends, transfer portal tracking, game-situation analysis.
* **Econometric Modeling:** Predictor engineering, probability analysis, efficiency metric mapping.
