### Project Overview
Examined whether elite college football teams can be identified using season-level performance metrics rather than reputation or narrative. 

### Technical Execution
* Utilized publicly available data from the `cfbfastR` package, analyzing teams from 2004–2025.
* Modeled total season wins as a function of offensive efficiency, defensive performance, and possession structure.
* Applied regression modeling and out-of-sample validation to verify the statistical profiles of elite programs.
