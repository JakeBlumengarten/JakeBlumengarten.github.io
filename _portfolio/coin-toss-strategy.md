### Project Overview
Evaluated the common strategic decision in college football—whether to receive the opening kickoff—using play-by-play data and statistical modeling. 

### Key Contributions
* Sourced, parsed, and cleaned multi-season college football play-by-play data in R.
* Built drive-level logistic regression models that proved **over 90% effective** at assessing scoring probabilities based on initial possession conditions.
* Generated interactive dashboard reports in R to deliver clear, data-driven game preparation guidance directly to football staff.
