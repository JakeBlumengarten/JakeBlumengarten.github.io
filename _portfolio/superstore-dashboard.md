### Project Overview
Built a decision-focused Tableau dashboard to analyze sales, profit, order volume, and shipping performance for a fictitious retail Superstore. 

### Key Insights & Execution
* **Revenue Drivers:** Broke down sales by sub-category, revealing that a small number of products (Phones, Chairs, Storage) drive a disproportionate share of revenue compared to long-tail items.
* **Profit vs. Volume:** Tracked average profit and order volume by year, demonstrating that higher volume does not automatically equate to higher margins.
* **Geographic Variances:** Developed a state-level profit map to expose meaningful geographic differences, identifying high-revenue states that underperform on profitability.
* **Operational Efficiency:** Surfaced shipping performance friction that actively eats into profit margins.
